About This Project

DJASSER ACHLAF

Developer & Creator

DJASSER ACHLAF programmed this project to make it easier to find blood donors in hospitals and any places where blood is needed, for saving lives at critical moments.

This platform connects donors with those in urgent need, because every second matters when someone's life is on the line. One registration can be the difference between life and death.
